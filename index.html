Indonesia Virtual Field Tour

Indonesia Virtual Field Tour is an Augmented Reality-based monopoly board game in the context of virtual tourism education in the New Normal Era. This application is the default application of the monopoly game set developed by Proper Team since 2021.

Virtual Field Tour Apk presents a 3D display of typical Indonesian icons with real-time interaction accompanied by a comprehensive explanation of Indonesian Tourism in your hands. Travel around Indonesia, visit various places and give your best ability to interact between the real and virtual worlds in the Indonesia Virtual Field Tour.

Release date : August 17th, 2021
Project type : Commission
My role : Game Programmer

My contributions

  1. Integrated a third-party tool, Vuforia, with Unity to detect augmented marker
  2. Applied augmented reality card narration reading
